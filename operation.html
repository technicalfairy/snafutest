OPERATION DETAILS

OPERATION $SNAFU is a strategic community initiative designed to reward the top 10,000 $SNAFU token holders when the market cap reaches $1 billion. A total of 10% of the token supply is reserved for an airdrop, which decreases by 1% each month until the $1 billion milestone is achieved. Once the market cap crosses the milestone, a snapshot will be taken within a one-week period, provided the market cap stays above the milestone. The airdrop will be distributed one month after the snapshot, with eligible addresses receiving their share based on the percentage of the total supply they own relative to the other top 10,000 addresses.

To claim the airdrop, addresses must retain at least 10% of their balance as it was at the time of the snapshot. Monthly reductions, unclaimed DEX pool rewards, and forfeited claims will remain in the airdrop pool for future community operations.
